About us

Cakes and Buns is a bakery business based in the lovely Land of Hornbills, and was founded by Jane Wong which is also the current owner of Cakes and Buns. The Cake and Buns strategy has always been to possess a deep understanding of the catering and casual eating market. Utilising 30 years of experience, our family owned and run business fully understand the need to produce quality food while maintaining prices at an affordable level.
